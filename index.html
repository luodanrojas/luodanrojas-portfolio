Hi, I'm...
A recent Northwestern University graduate from the Medill School of Journalism and Communications. I'm a big fan of anything design, film and journalism-related. I have a minor in environmental policy and try to learn as much as I can about the earth and its constant changes. Traveling is a passion of mine - I travel during my free time as much as I can. I studied in Taiwan and the Czech Republic and worked in Nicaragua, Ecuador and Panama.
